01

The Objective

Memorize, swap, and peek at your cards to minimize the total sum of your hand. The game ends when a player calls "CABO", thinking they have the lowest score, or when the deck is exhausted.

Target Score

100 PTS

Players

2-4 ARCHITECTS

02

Core Gameplay

I. Setup Phase

Each player receives four cards face down. You may peek at the outer two cards once. The remaining deck forms the draw pile.

II. The Turn Cycle

On your turn, you must perform one of three actions:

  • Draw from Deck Draw a card. Use its ability, swap it with one of your cards, or discard it.
  • Take from Discard Swap the top discard card with one of yours. You do not get the card's special ability.
  • Call Cabo End the round. Other players get one final turn.
03

The Kamikaze Rule

The Perfect Reset

If you end the round with exactly two 12s and two 13s, your score for that round is 0. All other players receive 50 penalty points.
